Slurm User Group Meeting 2023

The Slurm User Group Meeting (SLUG'23) this fall will be held in person at the Brigham Young University from September 12-13, 2023.

Sites interested in submitting technical talks or site reports are encourage to send a brief abstract to slugc@schedmd.com by June 9th. Notifications of acceptance will go out on June 23.
